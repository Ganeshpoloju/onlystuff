How onlyStuff works

Simple, safe, and built around your community.

Getting started

1

Sign in with Google

No username or password. Your Google account is all you need.

2

Upload your Aadhaar

We verify every user's Aadhaar card to make the platform safe and trustworthy. Verification takes up to 24 hours.

3

Enter your address

We automatically match you to your apartment community. If it's new, you can submit a request and we'll add it.

4

Start buying or selling

Browse your community's listings, create your own, or join a group buy to unlock better prices.

Buying

🔍

Community-first search

Results show your community's listings first, then nearby, then city-wide. Closer = better.

👥

Group Buying

Start or join a group buy on any listing. Once the target quantity is hit, the order locks and everyone gets the bulk price.

🚚

Flexible delivery

Pick up yourself, book a delivery partner, or arrange directly with the seller via chat.

⭐

Rate your experience

After every transaction, both buyer and seller can rate each other. Reviews are visible on profiles.

Selling

🏷️

Slab & MOQ pricing

Set a minimum order quantity and tiered pricing. Buyers who can't meet MOQ alone can start a group buy.

🗓️

Service scheduling

List services with time slots, buffer time, and recurring booking support. Your calendar, your control.

📊

Order management

Confirm orders, close them when done, and resolve disputes — all from your Sell tab.
