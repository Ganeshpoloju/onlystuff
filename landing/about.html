About onlyStuff

We're building the trust layer for neighbourhood commerce.

Why we built this

Hyderabad has thousands of apartment communities. Each one is a micro-economy — residents with things to sell, services to offer, and needs to fill. But when someone wants to sell their old laptop or find a reliable tutor, they're stuck posting on anonymous platforms where trust is hard to establish.

onlyStuff is different. We start with identity. Every user is Aadhaar-verified. Every transaction is between people who live near each other. When you buy from someone on onlyStuff, you know they're a real, verified resident — not an anonymous account.

We started in Hyderabad because it's home. Our goal is to make onlyStuff the default way apartment communities trade with each other.

Our principles

🛡️

Trust first

Identity verification isn't optional. Every user on onlyStuff is who they say they are.

🏘️

Community first

Local discovery, local transactions. Shorter distances, more trust, less friction.

🆓

Free to start

Zero commission at launch. We grow when our communities grow, not before.
